Electrical Design & Infrastructure

During my internship, I focused on the design and technical auditing of electrical systems for public buildings. This experience allowed me to apply engineering standards to real-world infrastructure, specifically through advanced AutoCAD implementation.

Electrical Systems & AutoCAD Design

During my internship at Pendik Municipality, I took an active role in transforming architectural blueprints into functional electrical projects by managing specialized layers for lighting, power, and low-current systems. My work involved designing comprehensive floor plans where I calculated Load Schedules to ensure phase balance and determined appropriate breaker values for high-power industrial equipment. Every design was verified through rigorous voltage drop and current-carrying capacity calculations, ensuring compliance with the 1.5% statutory limit for safety and efficiency.

Safety Systems & Infrastructure Auditing

A core component of my internship was engineering Earthing and Lightning Protection systems. Following the national earthing regulations, I designed earthing grids using galvanized strips and electrodes, calculating their placement to achieve a spread resistance of less than 2Ω to guarantee human safety. Furthermore, I conducted technical reviews of Reactive Power Compensation units and Solar Power Systems, focusing on their integration into urban grids to optimize energy performance.
