Hospital Automation & Technical Site

During my four-month tenure at the Kocaeli City Hospital project, I served as a Technical Site Lead, managing the deployment of advanced automation and infrastructure systems. Unlike a standard internship, this role involved full-time professional responsibility, where I moved rapidly from technical orientation to leading large-scale on-site operations. I was entrusted with the oversight of critical healthcare environments, ensuring that the hospital’s complex electronic and automation backbone met rigorous medical and industrial standards.

Technical Leadership & Operational Management

  • Workforce Supervision: I directly managed and coordinated a technical team of 10 to 15 specialized workers. My responsibilities included daily task delegation, quality control of installations, and ensuring that all on-site activities aligned with the master project timeline.
  • Operating Room (OR) Automation: I took a lead role in the installation and routing of automation systems for operating theaters. This included the calibration and control of differential air pressure systems and climate control units, which are critical for maintaining sterile environments and patient safety.
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU) Integration: I oversaw the technical wiring and device connectivity within ICUs and neonatal units, managing the integration between bedside medical sensors and the central hospital monitoring network.
  • Infrastructure & Panel Engineering: My early phase involved the systematic placement of automation control panels and the implementation of complex cable routing strategies (above-ceiling systems) across multiple floors, ensuring organized and serviceable infrastructure.

Mini Part of Hospital Automation Project:

Project Coordination & Corporate Representation

As a key member of the technical team, I represented my firm in high-level project tracking and coordination meetings. Being the primary person responsible for the automation progress on my assigned floors, I provided status reports, addressed technical bottlenecks, and collaborated with other engineering disciplines (mechanical, civil, and electrical) to ensure seamless system integration.

Professional Impact

This role significantly enhanced my leadership capabilities and technical expertise in Building Management Systems (BMS) and healthcare-specific automation. Managing a large team in a high-stakes environment like a city hospital sharpened my problem-solving skills, technical reporting accuracy, and my ability to deliver complex engineering solutions under intense operational pressure.
